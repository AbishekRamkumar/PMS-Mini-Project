A Few words about us

This is what tells about "who we are" and about pioneers of the institution

The K. R. College of Arts & Science was established by the benevolent philanthropist Thiru K. Ramasamy, the Chairman of National Educational and Charitable Trust, Kovilpatti. This trust was established in 1984-1985 under the Chairmanship of Thiru K. Ramasamy. The first educational institution originated from the trust was National Engineering College. The Chairman decided to start a self-financing Arts & Science College in the style and name of K. R. College of Arts & Science (Co-Education). The aim of this institution is to impart higher education to economically poor students in the vicinity of Kovilpatti. The Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli has granted affiliation to this college vide its Letter No. MSU/ CD/ Fresh-Aff/ 97-98 dated 22-08-1997. The College started functioning from 29-08-1997. Now, our college has completed 2 decades of academic service to rural students.

Placement Cell

Mould the students to Mould the society

Progress is the activity of today and assurance of tomorrow. Placement is one of the core herbs of any education. Training & Placement cell is an integral part of our college and is provided with a complete infrastructure for its effective functioning. The cell arranges campus interviews for direct recruitment besides conducting Aptitude test, Personality development programmes, Interpersonal Communication and entrepreneurship development programmes. Moreover, The Training & Placement Cell of our college is committed to provide all possible assistance and guidance to its learners in their efforts to find an employment. The Cell maintains a cordial relationship with all the recruiting companies.

Objectives

We Aim to

To mould the students to develop their overall personality through personality Development Programmes.
To enrich their interactive, communication and soft skills through periodic training to fulfil the basic requirement of renowned recruiters.
To upgrade their knowledge through Guest Lectures, Seminars, Training programmes and Value-Added courses for facing the challenges of latest technology and development.
To provide a suitable platform to the students' community for getting placement opportunities to find a job.
